FAQ

What is Elytse?
Elytse is a social fashion marketplace that connects fashion enthusiasts and style-conscious shoppers with independent fashion brands, designers, and influencers.
How does Elytse work?
Elytse offers a unique shopping experience that combines social networking with online shopping. Users can browse and shop for fashion items, connect with like-minded fashion enthusiasts, and even earn rewards and discounts through our gamification features.
Do you sell only clothes?
No!, We connect product and service providers of fashion which is related to clothing, beauty and appearance.
Do you provide services too?
Yes, we connect fashion service providers like Tailors, Shoe Makers, Models, Jewelers, Fashion Models, Make-Up Artists, Fashion Photographers, Fashion journalists, Influencers, Logistic Companies and Personnels and so on. Our goal is to provide a platform that offers a diverse selection of fashion services for our users.
What are the benefits of using Elytse?
Elytse offers a lot of unique experience that combines social networking with online shopping. Users can connect with like-minded fashion enthusiasts, discover new independent brands and designers, and even make money through Sell and Swap Feature and earn rewards and discounts through our gamification features.
How can I contact Elytse customer support?
You can contact Elytse customer support through our website or mobile app. We have a dedicated team of customer support representatives that are available to assist you with any questions or concerns.
